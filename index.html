Location

Beckenham Place Park is Lewisham's largest green space, extending to 96 hectares (237 acres), and including a wide range of facilities, including a sensory garden.

A railway bisects the park and the only routes joining both parts are the bridge north of woodland called the Ash Plantation or by leaving and re-entering the park in the vicinity of Ravensbourne railway station. Hence, the railway effectively divides the park into west and east sides.

Beckenham Place Park is considered one of the finest wildlife sites in the borough, containing ancient woodland as well as a variety of other habitats. Notable trees include oak, wild service tree, sweet chestnut and an ancient Turkey oak tree (reputedly the second oldest in Britain), that survived an arson attack in the 1990s but succumbed to wind, old age and vandalism in 2002. There is also an old mulberry tree.

The park underwent a £6.8 million regeneration in 2019, which saw the closure of the public golf course, and opening of a wild swimming lake, BMX track and tennis courts.

It is a Local Nature Reserve and a Site of Metropolitan Importance for Nature Conservation It is also part of the Green Chain Walk and Capital Ring.

Facilities

Tennis Surfaces

All our courts offer a range of surfaces all year round. They include classic hard court options such as synthetic clay and article grass to name a few, all expertly chosen to enhance your tennis experience.

Play Tennis

Our mission is to cater for all levels and provide everything you need to improve your game and build confidence. Whether you are a complete beginner or an experienced player there really is no better way to get started.

Kids Tennis

Our programme offers group classes and individual lessons for all ages whether your child is new to tennis or a teenager ready to take their game to the next level. It is our commitment to help all tennis players to realise their potential, make new friends and have lots of fun.

Tennis Lessons

All our classes and lessons will help you learn more about the game, develop new skills and build your fitness. Our highly experienced tennis coaches will help advance your performance and improve your technique. Our lessons are also a great way to meet fellow budding tennis players with a shared love and interest in the game.

Gym

Our friendly, open-planned gym boasts a comprehensive range of cutting-edge equipment in a spacious, clean environment divided into specialised zones. Our brilliant intro sessions are led by a dedicated fitness team, allowing you to set your goals with a personalised fitness plan - whatever your aim.

Clubroom

In collaboration with chefs’, nutritionists and fitness experts, we have created a delicious food and drinks menu for whatever time of day. We provide a dedicated adult only area for you to use at your pleasure whether it is reading the papers with a coffee, catching up with business or meeting a friend at the bar.
